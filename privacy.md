# Privacy Policy for Gross Income

**Last Updated:** December 31, 2025

**Gross Income** ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. We understand that the data you track in this application is personal, and we are committed to ensuring it remains private.

## 1. No Data Collection
**We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data.**

* **Local Storage Only:** All entries, timers, earnings calculations, and logs created within Gross Income are stored locally on your device.
* **No Cloud Sync:** We do not send your data to any external servers.
* **No Analytics:** We do not track how you use the app or which features you interact with.

## 2. Third-Party Sharing
Because we do not collect any data, we have no data to share with third parties, advertisers, or marketing agencies.

## 3. Data Retention & Deletion
* **Retention:** Your data stays on your device as long as the app is installed.
* **Deletion:** If you delete the Gross Income app from your device, all data associated with it is permanently deleted immediately. We cannot recover this data for you because we do not have a copy of it.
